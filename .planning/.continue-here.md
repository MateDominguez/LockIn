## Context & Approach

**Project Philosophy:**
- Transparencia total (core value) — every decision must be explainable
- EU AI Act compliance from day 1 (not bolted on later)
- Value Investing methodology (Graham, Greenwald, Dalio) via RAG
- Dialectical architecture (Bulls debate Bears, Judge arbitrates)
- HITL when needed, not constant interruption

**Technical Approach:**
- Start small, prove architecture works (vertical slice in Phase 2)
- Agents as pure functions on shared state (auditability)
- Point-in-time data access to avoid look-ahead bias
- Temperature=0 for determinism (reproducible debugging)
- Risk-based HITL triggering (avoid fatigue)

**User Profile:**
- TFM student, deadline June 2026
- Limited budget (using free tiers)
- Spanish-speaking (but MVP in English OK)
- Values transparency over performance
- Interactive mode (wants to review plans before execution)
